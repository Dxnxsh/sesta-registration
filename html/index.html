School History



The competition to achieve excellence in the world of education demands the birth of another Full Boarding School (SBP) in the state of Perak Darul Ridzuan. The existence of another SBP in the Batang Padang District brings the desire to continue the leap of SBP excellence in this country. The school named Sekolah Menengah Sains Tapah in conjunction with its location in Tapah town is located on Jalan Pahang about 4 kilometers from Tapah Town and is the main route to Cameron Highland.

The Tapah Science High School construction project started on October 12, 2006, and was scheduled to be completed on November 11, 2008. However, due to unavoidable problems, the project could only be completed on August 29, 2009. The project costing RM38,482,000.00 includes various facilities for boarding schools. Tapah Science High School covers a land area of ​​30 acres.

During the construction period of this school project, Mr. Zulkarnain bin Zainal Abidin was appointed as the caretaker effective March 2009 . When he arrived at this school, he found that there were still many construction works that had not yet been completed. For that reason, the ministry has ordered Mr Zulkarnain bin Zainal Abidin to return to duty at Tunku Abdul Rahman School (STAR) Ipoh, Perak Darul Ridzuan to continue his responsibilities as Senior Administrative Assistant. On June 16, 2009 he was once again directed to this school and at that time only about 10% of the construction work could not be completed. This situation forces Encik Zulkarnain bin Zainal Abidin to go back and forth from Ipoh to Tapah because as a 'caretaker' , he is responsible for managing various matters related to the opening of a school. Among his duties are managing school registration, the placement of teachers and school support staff, receiving supplies of school furniture and equipment and matters related to finances and school accounts. In addition, he is also responsible for managing the cleaning service of the school grounds.

The project handover ceremony for this completed school was held on 23 October 2009 by the contractor to Puan Hajjah Norlia binti Mohd Shuhaili as the Deputy Director representing the Boarding School and Cluster School Division Director representing the Ministry of Education Malaysia. He then handed over this school to Encik Zulkarnain bin Zainal Abidin as the main leader of Tapah Science Secondary School (SESTA).

When the Tapah Science High School was handed over, school furniture and equipment began to be received from December 2009. Meanwhile, to control the safety of the school, a security control company was appointed to carry out the duties from 1 February 2010.

The first principal assigned to lead Tapah Science Secondary School was Encik Hadi bin Mat Dain from 1 February 2010. He has extensive experience in the world of SBP education as he has served at Tengku Abdullah Science Secondary School (SEMESTA), Raub Pahang and Integrated Boarding School ( SBPI) Temerloh, Pahang. He was also assisted by three senior assistant teachers namely Mr Zulkarnain Bin Zainal Abidin as Senior Administrative Assistant, Mr Asma Azmin Rashid Bin Mohd Nawawi as Senior HEM Assistant and Mr Zulkipli Bin Abd. Latip as Co-Curriculum Senior Assistant. Until March 2010, this school has received the presence of 35 teachers.

FAQs

Frequently Ask Questions

What is SESTA

SESTA abreviation for Sekolah Menengah Sains Tapah

Who is eligible to register to SESTA

Eligibility criteria for SESTA registration include for students who wishes to continue their studies in Form 1 or Form 4

How can I access the SESTA platform?

Accessing the SESTA platform involves going through our website

What steps are involved in the registration process?

The registration process includes creating an account via filling in personal details for the system to create your account after pressing the sign up button at the login page.

Can parents/guardians register multiple children under one account?

Yes, parents/guardians can register multiple children but has the create an account for each of them

What information and documents are required for registration?

To complete registration, you'll need the student infomation, parent/guardian infomation.

How do I update my personal information after registration?

To update your personal information, you have to inform our system adminstrator who will update your infomation.

What should I do if I forget my password

If you forget your password or username, you can recover them by pressing "forget password?" button at the login page.

Is there a mobile app for SESTA, or is it accessible on mobile browsers?

SESTA is accessible on mobile browsers only and web browsers on pc.

Can I track the status of my registration?

Yes, you can track the status of your registration by login in the account itself to see if your registration was successfully completed.

How do I pay registration fees through SESTA?

To pay registration fees through SESTA, follow these steps

1)Login your account.
2)Go to "Billing".
3)You can view the status payment of your billing.
4)Choose the payment that you wanted to pay then click proceed payment.
5)Confirm the payment
6)The system will provide you with QR code and number account bank to transfer the money to.
7)Users need to upload the receipt of the payment.
8)Users can view, print and view email receipt.
9)Status of the payment will change to pending.

Are there multiple payment options available?

Yes, SESTA provides two payment options which is QR code and transfer online via fpx (number account bank will be shown when you confirm the payment).

What security measures are in place to protect my personal data?

SESTA employs various security measures to protect your personal data, by having the data administrator

How can I withdraw my registration if needed?

To withdraw your registration, just contact the support system.

Are there any specific deadlines for completing the registration process?

Yes, there are specific deadlines for completing the registration process, which are two weeks before the orientation day.

Can I register for classes or extracurricular activities through SESTA?

No, you can't register for classes or extracurricular activities through SESTA because the administrator will assigned you to the class based on your performance.

Is there a user guide or tutorial available for SESTA?

Yes, SESTA provides a user guide and tutorial to assist users in the chatbox of the system. Users can just WhatsApp to ask anything about anything.

How can I contact technical support for assistance?

To contact technical support for assistance, users can just contact we in the chatbox

What do I do if I encounter technical issues during the registration process?

If you encounter technical issues during the registration process, try contact the technical support system in the chatbox.
